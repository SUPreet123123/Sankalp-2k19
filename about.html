PARAM

PARAM means "SUPREME" in Sanskrit language. PARAM is a series of supercomputers designed and assembled by the Centre for Development of Advanced Computing (C-DAC) in Pune, India. After the launch of India's First Supercomputer PARAM 8000, India started getting recognition in the field of technology and advancement. Coming to our techno-management fest SANKLAP 2K19; there are 5 pillars of PARAM:

VEGA: We will conduct events which will determine "SPEED" of the participants.

NIPUNATA: We will conduct events which will determine "ACCURACY" of the participants.

BAHUMUKH PRATIBHA: We will conduct events which will determine "VERSATILITY" of the participants.

KARMISTATA: We will conduct events which will determine "DILIGENCE" of the participants.

KENDRABINDU: We will conduct events which will determine "FOCUS" of the participants.

All the events that will be organised by different clubs are classified according to these pillars of PARAM. We believe that SANKALP 2k19 will bring a new revolution in NIST and will ignite the spark among the NIST family.
